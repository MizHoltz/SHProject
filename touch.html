Application Interaction
Mouse

Using a mouse is the easitest way to interact with computers. It has been around for such a long time, that it's secound nature. There are many actions with the mouse such as the "click" and moving the cursor around.

Touch

The touch event is the mouse event but using your fingers. The touch interface has the user touch at a touchpoint to interact with the device, mostly with tablets and phones. The touch event can do many actions like scrolling up or down and zooming in and out. Although not as well versed like the mouse, it has become very popular and well adjusted.

Pointer

Pointer events are used with tools like the apple pen to replace or enhance the touch event. It involves using a device to point at an object. It's used to organize the mouse and touch events. You have to apply pressure in order to activate the pointer.
